Important product logic:

* Booking, patient lookup, and availability checking are fully automated through Juvonno.
* Cancellation and rescheduling are request-based and require staff notification.
* Clinics can edit safe settings such as FAQs, clinic hours, greetings, SMS templates, transfer numbers, and notification recipients.
* NAP admins control Juvonno API credentials, provider mappings, service mappings, Retell agent settings, booking logic, and webhooks.
* This is not a conversation builder.
* This is an AI receptionist control center, call analytics dashboard, and clinic operations portal.
